Our Story

PatuKrishi-Pro is a digital agriculture platform designed to help Indian farmers with modern, easy-to-use tools. It offers features like crop advisory based on region and season, photo-based soil fertility checks, weather and climate forecasting, mandi price updates, and an AI-powered Krishi Bot that answers farming questions in English with voice support.

It also includes a Lens tool for scanning and translating text into regional languages, making information more accessible.

Available in Standard and Pro versions with free technical support and a three-year guarantee, PatuKrishi-Pro focuses on bringing technology directly to farmers' hands to improve productivity, planning, and decision-making. [Future assumptions]
